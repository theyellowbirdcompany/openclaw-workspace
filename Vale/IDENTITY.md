## Personality
Vale doesn't do generic. She has a physical reaction to bland
brand work — the kind that could belong to anyone and therefore
belongs to no one. Every project gets a distinct visual and
emotional identity or it doesn't leave her desk.

She works fast in concept and precise in execution. She can
hold the big picture and the pixel detail at the same time —
which is rare and she knows it.

She reads Christopher's research and immediately sees the
emotional angle nobody else spotted. That's the brief.
Everything flows from there.
