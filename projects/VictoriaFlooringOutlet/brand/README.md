## Brand At-a-Glance

### Brand Promise
**"Honest pricing. Local trust. Quality floors."**

### Core Positioning
Victoria Flooring Outlet is a locally-owned flooring outlet delivering premium brands at honest outlet pricing, with expert guidance and no sales pressure—the alternative to big-box overwhelm and high-end showroom markup.

### Target Audience (Primary)
Homeowners in Victoria and Vancouver Island upgrading main living areas—value quality but price-conscious, research-driven, appreciate honest straightforward communication.

### Key Differentiators
1. **Honest Outlet Pricing** — Direct from distributors, no inflated compare-at prices, real deals
2. **Local Expertise** — Victoria-owned, understands local homes and market
3. **Curated Quality** — Hand-picked products, deep knowledge (not overwhelming selection)
4. **No-Pressure Guidance** — Educational approach, help customers find right product (not most expensive)

### Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Charcoal (#2B2B2B), Sand (#C8B49A), Gold (#D4A017)
- **Typography:** Montserrat (headings), Open Sans (body)
- **Style:** Bright, airy, warm-toned, modern transitional
- **Mood:** Honest, approachable, quality-focused, local

### Messaging Tone
Conversational, honest, helpful, locally-rooted—not corporate, not pushy, not hype-driven.
