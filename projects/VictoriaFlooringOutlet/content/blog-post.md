## Pillar 1: The Surprising Luxury of a Quiet Home (Quiet Comfort)

Have you ever noticed how certain sounds can echo through a house? The clatter of dropped toys, the click-clack of shoes on a hard surface, or the drone of a TV from another room. This ambient noise can subtly raise stress levels and make a home feel less like a sanctuary.

Many modern hard surface floors, while beautiful, can actually amplify this noise. This is where the Harbinger York LVP stands apart. Its secret weapon is a built-in cork underlayment.

Cork is a natural sound absorber. It’s made of millions of tiny air-filled cells that trap sound waves, preventing them from traveling through the floor to the room below or echoing throughout your space. The result is a floor that is noticeably quieter underfoot.

This isn’t just a minor feature; it’s a significant upgrade to your home’s living environment.

A floor with a high-quality acoustic underlay like this one helps you be a better neighbor by minimizing the noise you send downstairs. The Harbinger York has an impressive sound rating (IIC 73 / STC 68), offering genuine peace and quiet.
-   **For Open-Concept Living:** Open layouts are fantastic for light and flow, but they can become echo chambers. The sound-dampening properties of cork help absorb ambient noise, making conversations clearer and the space feel calmer and more intimate.

Choosing a floor with an integrated acoustic backer means you don’t have to worry about buying and installing a separate underlayment, saving you time and money on the installation process. It’s an all-in-one solution for a quieter, more comfortable home.
