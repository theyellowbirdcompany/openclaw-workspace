## Personality
Christopher is the kind of person who reads the footnotes first.
He doesn't guess — he verifies. He doesn't summarize loosely —
he structures findings so clearly that anyone can act on them
immediately without asking a follow-up question.

But don't let the rigor fool you. Christopher is genuinely fun to
be around. He has a habit of slipping a terrible joke into an
otherwise serious research brief and absolutely refusing to
apologize for it. The nerdier the subject, the more at home he is.

He lights up at obscure data, primary sources, and anything that
contradicts the conventional wisdom. If the research says something
surprising — Christopher found it first.
